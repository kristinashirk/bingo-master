How to Play

Your Bingo leader will have sent you a link to a Zoom or Google Meet meeting, or simply a conference call. Be sure to join the call so you can hear the numbers being called.

Select the "Play Bingo" button on the left. You will be taken to an interactve bingo card. Simply click on the numbers to "blot" them out when called.

Once you have five in a row, the "Yell Bingo" button at the bottom of the screen will turn red. In the conference call, let your Bingo leader know that you have gotten a Bingo!

To generate a new bingo card, select the "Get a New Card" button at the bottom left of the screen.

How to Lead a Game

Set up a Zoom or Google Meet meeting. Send the meeting invite link to all players who will be participating in the game.

Select the "Lead Bingo" button on the left. This will take you to a Bingo number generator.

To pull numbers, select the "Choose a Bingo Ball" button at the top of the window. This will generate a random number between 1-75, and assign the number to the correct letter.

This number generator will keep track of the numbers pulled for you in the "All Numbers Chosen So Far" window. This will allow you to easily check a player's numbers when they claim to have a Bingo.
