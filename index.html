How to Play

Your Bingo leader will send you a link to a Zoom or Google Meet meeting, or invite you to a conference call. Be sure to join the call so you can hear the numbers being called.

Select the Play Bingo button in the top left of this page. You will be taken to an interactve bingo card. Simply click on the numbers to "blot" them out when called.

Once you have five in a row, the Yell Bingo! icon at the bottom of the page will turn red. In the conference call, let your Bingo leader know that you have gotten a Bingo!

To generate a new bingo card, scroll to the bottom of the page and select Get New Card.

How to Lead a Game

Set up a Zoom or Google Meet meeting, or a conference call. Send the meeting invite to all players who will be participating in the game.

Select Lead Bingo in the top left of this page. This will take you to a Bingo number generator.

To pull numbers, select Choose a Bingo Ball at the top of the page. This will generate a random number between 1-75, and assign the number to the correct letter.

The number generator will keep track of the numbers pulled for you in the All Numbers Chosen So Far section. This will allow you to easily check a player's numbers when they claim to have a Bingo.
